Fried Rice and Chicken garnished with pepper sauce

Ingredients:

  • Basmati Rice
  • 1/2 Kilo of Chicken breast
  • Virgin Olive Oil
  • Tumeric | Ginger | Stock cube | Salt | Garlic | Nutmeg | Every Other Spices of your choice
  • Veggies[Carrot | Green peas | Green Beans | RED, GREEN, YELLOW Bell pepper]

Method of preparation:

  1. Wash and spice the chicken pieces.Boil until tender,then fry or grill them.
  2. Chop the carrot, cabbage, green pepper and green beans.
  3. Parboil the rice, then cook in the chicken stock (add 2 extra cups of water).
    Cook until almost tender, then set aside.
  4. Put a clean frying pan on fire. Add groundnut oil and onion to the pan.
    Stir for a minute then add the carrot and green beans.
    Stir for another minute.
  5. Add the cabbage, green pepper, white pepper, curry, seasoning and salt to taste (try to keep it a bit spicy).
    Stir-fry for 2 minutes.
  6. Add the rice to the mix, stir and simmer for 5 minutes.
    Serve with the chicken.
