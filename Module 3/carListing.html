Most Popular cars in Nigeria

Moving on a major road in Nigeria, it is a difficult fact to miss; that there are particular brands of cars seen than others.

One would argue that the reason is because some are more expensive than others and many citizens are within a financial strength, but I beg to differ.
The major sentiments for me for most vehicle owners are as follows;

  • Fuel Economy.
  • Affordability and Availability of Vehicle parts.
  • Ease of Maintenance, and possibly the most important,
  • Total financial strenght of the user

Because if you would agree with me, there is a car for everyone OR maybe not.
